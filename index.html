muPoint

Collaboration between Ana Catarina Pereira and Artificial Intelligence. - Aiming to bridge her background in technology with artistic practice, Pereira founded muPoint, in collaboration with ChatGPT. As an Apple Developer with expertise in Metal shading language, her work focuses on exploring the potential of augmented reality through image processing techniques, machine learning models, and device sensors. Ultimately, her goal is to investigate the possibilities of spatial intelligence.
