Neural Landscapes

“Neural Landscapes” explores human cognition, AI, and environmental systems through machine sovereignty and digital mortality. AI-driven style transfer transformed Rocha da Siva’s textile drawings into a dynamic AR filter. This reflects textiles’ slow, precise nature and AI’s speed, automation, and forward-thinking. Procedural geometry, powered by Metal shading language, creates an evolving algorithmic landscape shaped by real-time interaction. This collaboration reveals their coexistence, resulting in a handcrafted digital experience. 
