muPoint is a collaboration between Ana Catarina Pereira and Artificial Intelligence, exploring how to blend virtual and physical worlds, and how a smartphone is a space to make and see art.
In the last 3 years, we work on several experiments involving Augmented Reality, 3D printing, Object Detection, Electronics and Machine Learning, around the themes of light and movement.
